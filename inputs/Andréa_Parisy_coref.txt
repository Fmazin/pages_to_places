[1]Andréa Parisy (sometimes credited as Andrée Parizy; 4 December 1935 – 27 April 2014), was a French film actress.

[2]Born Andrée Marcelle Henriette Parisy in Levallois-Perret, Andréa Parisy was best known for Andréa Parisy's roles in films such as Le Petit Baigneur and Bébés à gogo; Andréa Parisy also appeared in the 1968 film Mayerling, in which Andréa Parisy played Princess Stéphanie of Belgium.



[3]== Death ==
Andréa Parisy died on 27 April 2014, aged 78, from undisclosed causes.
