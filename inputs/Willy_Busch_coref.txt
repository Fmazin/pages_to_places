[1]Wilhelm "Willy" Busch (4 January 1907 – 4 March 1982) was a German international footballer.
[2]



[3]==
[4]Club career =
[5]=
Willy Busch was a player of TuS Duisburg.
[6]From 1940 to 1943 Willy Busch played with Toni Turek, the 1954 World Champion.
[7]And in the 1943–44 season Willy Busch played in the final round of the German championship.
[8]



[9]==
[10]International career =
[11]=
Willy Busch won 13 cap for the Germany national football team, and took part in the 1934 FIFA World Cup.
