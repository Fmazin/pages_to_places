[1]Gergely Kulcsár (10 March 1934 – 12 August 2020) was a Hungarian javelin thrower.
[2]Gergely Kulcsár competed at the 1960, 1964, 1968 and 1972 Olympics and won two bronze medals, in 1960 and 1968, and a silver medal in 1964.
[3]Gergely Kulcsár was the Olympic flag bearer for Hungary in 1964, 1968, and 1972.
[4]

[5]Gergely Kulcsár was the first Hungarian to throw over 80 meters and won the national title eight times.
[6]Between 1975 and 1980 Gergely Kulcsár was the coach of the Hungarian national throwing team.
[7]Gergely Kulcsár trainees included Miklós Németh, a 1976 Olympic champion.
[8]From 1981 to 1993 Gergely Kulcsár coached the national throwing team of Kuwait.
